# You've Been Using LinkedIn Wrong All Along: How To Finally Change Your Profile URL and Boost Your Career!

LinkedIn is one of the most popular and powerful professional networking platforms available today. It enables professionals to connect and engage with their colleagues, peers, and employers in many ways. However, most users are unaware of the platform's hidden features, one of which is customizing their profile URL.

Have you ever noticed that your LinkedIn profile URL is long and full of random characters and numbers? This default URL not only looks unprofessional but also makes it difficult for others to find and connect with you. Thankfully, you can easily customize your LinkedIn profile URL to make it look more professional and memorable.

Here's how you can change your LinkedIn profile URL in just a few simple steps:

Step 1: Open your LinkedIn account and log in.

Step 2: Click on the "Me" icon at the top of the page.

Step 3: Select "View Profile" from the drop-down menu.

Step 4: Look for the "Edit public profile & URL" option on your profile page and click on it.

Step 5: On the right side of the page, you'll see a section marked "Edit your custom URL." Click on the "Edit" button next to it.

Step 6: Type your desired URL in the text box provided. We recommend using your full name, without any spaces or special characters.

Step 7: Once you're done, click on the "Save" button at the bottom of the page.

Congratulations! You've successfully customized your LinkedIn profile URL. Your new URL should be shorter, cleaner, and more memorable than the default one. You can now share it with your colleagues, peers, and potential employers to help them find and connect with you on LinkedIn.

Why Customizing Your LinkedIn Profile URL Matters?

Now that you've learned how to customize your LinkedIn profile URL, you might be wondering why it's essential. Here are a few reasons why customizing your LinkedIn profile URL matters:

1. Makes You Memorable: A memorable LinkedIn profile URL can help others easily remember your profile and find you again in the future. It also creates a professional and cohesive brand image for your online presence.

2. Improves Your SEO: Customizing your LinkedIn profile URL can improve your search engine optimization (SEO). When someone searches for you online, your LinkedIn profile will appear higher in search results, making it easier for them to find you.

3. Boosts Your Career: A professional and memorable LinkedIn profile URL can make a strong first impression on potential employers and recruiters. It can also help you stand out in a crowded job market and lead to new career opportunities.

In conclusion, customizing your LinkedIn profile URL is a quick and easy way to boost your online presence and career prospects. By following the steps outlined above, you can create a professional and memorable URL that will help you stand out on LinkedIn. So why wait? ## Steps to Change Your LinkedIn Profile URL (Desktop & Mobile)


So, in this article, we will share a step-by-step guide on how to change your LinkedIn profile URL. To change the LinkedIn profile’s web address, you can use the web version of LinkedIn or its mobile app.

 
### 1) Changing LinkedIn Profile URL on Desktop


Changing LinkedIn profile URL on a desktop is pretty easy; you need to use your favorite web browser. Here’s what you need to do.
1. First of all, launch your favorite web browser and open the Linkedin website. Next, sign in to your account.
2. On the top right corner, click on the drop-down icon behind Me and select the ‘View Profile’ option.

3. Click on the Edit public profile & URL option on the right sidebar.

4. Next, click on the pencil icon behind your Profile URL, as shown in the screenshot below.

5. You will be able to edit your Profile URL. You need to clear the existing URL and type the new URL of your choice. Once done, click on the Save button.


 
### 2) Changing LinkedIn Profile URL on Mobile


Just like the desktop version, you can use the mobile app of LinkedIn to change your Profile URL. Here’s what you need to do.
1. First of all, open the LinkedIn mobile app on your Android, iPhone, or iPad. Next, tap on the profile picture at the top-left corner.

2. Next, tap on the View Profile option as shown below.

3. Now scroll down and find the Contact section. Under the Contact, tap on the Edit icon as shown below.

4. Next, tap on your Profile URL.

5. Now click on the Pencil icon behind the Edit your custom URL option.

6. You can now edit the profile link. Type a new URL for your Profile and tap on the Save button.

That’s it! You are done. This is how you can use the LinkedIn mobile app to change the profile URL.
